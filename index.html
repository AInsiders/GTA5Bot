Help & How to Play

Quick start

  1. Invite the bot ‚Äî Ask your server admin to add GTA Bot, or use an invite from the support server.
  2. Accept the Terms ‚Äî In Discord, run /start and accept the Terms of Service to create your character.
  3. Open the main menu ‚Äî Run /menu to see your wallet, bank, and all activities (Jobs, Shop, Bank, Heists, etc.).
  4. Earn and protect ‚Äî Do jobs or heists to earn cash, then use /bank to deposit money so it can't be stolen.
  5. Get in-Discord help ‚Äî Use /help in any server with the bot for the full help menu.

First Steps

  • Use /menu ‚Äî Access the main game interface
  • Check your profile ‚Äî See your current stats
  • Start with easy jobs ‚Äî Build your reputation

Understanding Money

  • Wallet Money ‚Äî Cash you carry (can be stolen)
  • Bank Money ‚Äî Protected funds (safe from theft)
  • Chips ‚Äî Casino currency (buy/sell at Cashier's Cage)
  • Net Worth ‚Äî Total value of all your assets

Your Stats

  • Cash ‚Äî Money in your wallet
  • Reputation ‚Äî Your criminal standing
  • Rank ‚Äî Your criminal title
  • Wanted Level ‚Äî How much heat you're carrying

Dual Currency System

  • Wallet Money ‚Äî Stealable by other players
  • Bank Money ‚Äî Cannot be stolen
  • Keep spending money in wallet, savings in bank

Banking Commands

  • /bank ‚Äî Open banking interface
  • /deposit <amount> ‚Äî Deposit wallet to bank
  • /withdraw <amount> ‚Äî Withdraw bank to wallet

Basics

  • Target wallet only ‚Äî Bank money is protected
  • Players need $500+ in wallet to be targeted
  • Cooldowns: 30 minutes between attempts

Commands

  • /steal @player ‚Äî Regular theft (60% success)
  • /mug @player ‚Äî High-risk mugging (higher reward)

Full details for Jobs, Heists, Business, Casino, and Shop are available in the in-Discord /help menu. Use /menu to access all activities.

How do I start?

Run /start, accept the Terms, then /menu.

Can my bank be robbed?

No. Only wallet cash can be stolen. Keep savings in the bank.

Where do I get help?

Use /help in Discord or join the Support server.
