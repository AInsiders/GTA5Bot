Help & How to Play

Follow the steps below, then use the guide for details.

Quick start instructions

  1. Invite the bot — Ask your server admin to add GTA Bot, or use an invite from the support server.
  2. Accept the Terms — In Discord, run /start and accept the Terms of Service to create your character.
  3. Open the main menu — Run /menu to see your wallet, bank, and all activities (Jobs, Shop, Bank, Heists, etc.).
  4. Earn and protect — Do jobs or heists to earn cash, then use /bank to deposit money so it can’t be stolen.
  5. Get in-Discord help — Use /help in any server with the bot for the full help menu.

How do I start playing?

Run /start in a server that has the bot, accept the Terms of Service, then use /menu to open the main interface. From there you can do jobs, bank, shop, and more.

How do I invite the bot to my server?

You need the bot invite link from the bot owner or the support server. Server admins can add the bot there; regular users can join the support server to play and get help.

What is RP / Reputation?

RP (Reputation) is your criminal standing. You earn it from jobs, heists, and other activities. Higher rep unlocks better heists and can make you harder to steal from. Your level is based on total RP (GTA Online–style progression).

Can my bank be robbed?

No. Money in your bank cannot be stolen. Only wallet (cash) can be taken by other players via /steal or /mug. Keep most of your money in the bank and only carry what you need.

What are cooldowns?

Many actions (jobs, heists, stealing, daily reward) have a cooldown—a wait time before you can do them again. The bot will tell you how long is left. Use /menu to see your stats and try again when ready.

Where do I get help in Discord?

Use /help in any server with the bot for the in-game help menu with categories. For bugs, suggestions, or community help, join the GTA Bot Support server.

Is the bot free? Is it real money?

The bot is for entertainment; all in-game currency and items are virtual and have no real-world value. There is no real-money gambling or trading. Check your server for any server-specific rules.

Why can’t I use a command?

You must accept the Terms first (/start). Some commands are restricted (e.g. /setlevelingchannel is Server Owner only, /reset-tos is admin). If the bot says “database unavailable,” try again later.
