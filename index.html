What You Can Do

Run jobs, heists, and businesses; bank your cash; climb the leaderboards.

  • 💼 Jobs (Robbery, Hitman, Racing, Car Theft) Run criminal jobs to earn cash and build reputation. Cooldowns and payouts vary by job type.
  • 🎭 Heists (Quick & Major) Quick heists for fast payouts or major heists for big scores. Team up or go solo from Heist Central.
  • 🏢 Businesses (MC, Nightclub, Specialty) Own and run MCs, nightclubs, and specialty businesses. Restock, deliver, and earn passive income.
  • 📦 Warehouses (Cargo & Vehicles) Source and sell cargo or steal and store vehicles. Manage warehouses for steady criminal income.
  • 🎰 Casino & Mini-Games Play blackjack, slots, and mini-games. Earn chips at the Cashier's Cage; risk it for big wins.
  • 🏦 Banking & Protection Deposit cash to protect it from theft. Daily rewards and streak bonuses keep your empire growing.
  • 🔫 Stealing (PvP) Mug or steal from other players' wallets. Bank money is safe; only carried cash can be taken.
  • 📊 Leveling & Leaderboards Earn XP from activities, rank up your criminal title, and compete on global leaderboards.

Full Guide

Click a topic card to expand and read. Scroll inside the card to see all content. Everything about how GTA Bot works: starting, jobs, hustle, weapon draw, properties, restrictions, and all systems.

How to Begin

  1. Make sure GTA Bot is in your Discord server (ask an admin or use the invite link on this site).
  2. In any channel where the bot can read and send messages, run /start.
  3. Read the Terms of Service, then click Accept Terms to create your character.
  4. Use /menu to open the main interface.

What /start Does

  • First time: Shows a welcome message and TOS summary with buttons Accept Terms and View Full Terms. Accepting creates your character and gives you starting cash ($5,000+) and chips (1,000+).
  • Already accepted: Opens the main menu (same as /menu).

Before / After /start

Until you accept the Terms, only /start works; every other command shows a message telling you to use /start. After you accept, all commands and menus are available.

First Commands

/menu (main hub), /profile (stats), /help (in-Discord help). From the main menu you get buttons for Jobs, Bank, Steal, Business, Shop, Profile, Leaderboards, Casino, Help.

Understanding Money

Wallet — Cash you carry (can be stolen). Bank — Protected funds (safe from theft). Chips — Casino currency; buy/sell at the Cashier's Cage. Net Worth — Total value of all your assets. /hustle — Street cash every 3s; base $2–$300 × multiplier (more weapon levels = more payout).

Your Progress

Cash, RP (Reputation) — drives your Rank. Rank — derived from total RP. Wanted Level — heat from crimes; affects some odds.

Protection Strategy

Keep spending money in wallet; deposit savings in bank. Stay below the theft threshold (check Street Crime → Protection in /menu).

If You're Stuck

Run /start and click Accept Terms. Use /menu then Jobs or Bank. Use /help in Discord or join the Support server.

Open /menu → Jobs (or /jobs). All jobs give cash and often RP; each has its own cooldown. This is your main early-game loop.

Job rewards

Cash payouts (primary income), RP (drives Rank), wanted level (some jobs raise heat). Harder = better pay usually.

Robberies

  • Easy: $1K–$1.5K | ~80% success | 1⭐ wanted | 5 min CD
  • Medium: $1.5K–$2.5K | ~75% success | 2⭐ wanted | 10 min CD
  • Hard: $2.5K–$3K | ~70% success | 3⭐ wanted | 20 min CD

Hitman

Reflex mini-game. Easy $1.5K–$3K (10 min CD), Medium $3K–$6K (15 min CD), Hard $6K–$12K (30 min CD). Gain reputation.

Racing

You pay entry fee; winner gets payout. Lose = lose the fee. Street $300/$1K win (5 min CD), Circuit $800/$3K (10 min), Grand Prix $2K/$8K (20 min).

Car Theft

Lock-picking: select pin 1–4, move Up/Down to align. All four pins must turn green to unlock; 100+ cars; dynamic difficulty; 30 min cooldown.

Trivia

Quiz-based job (if enabled).

Tips & risks

Start Easy to build rep; manage cooldowns. Failed jobs can cost money and rep.

/hustle — Pick up street cash ($2–$300 per claim). No target; works in any server. Cooldown is enforced across all guilds and instances (atomic).

  • Payout: Base $2–$300 (configurable). Your total weapon levels multiply the payout — more levels = higher hustle income.
  • Cooldown: 3 seconds between claims, shared globally.
  • When to use: Quick filler between jobs and heists.

/weapondraw @opponent — Challenge another player to a Best 3 of 5 duel. Optional cash wager; bragging rights.

How It Works

  1. You send the challenge (optional wager in cash). The opponent can Accept Bet or Decline; others can Challenge Instead; you can Cancel.
  2. If someone else requests to fight instead, you can Accept Alt or Keep Waiting for the original target.
  3. Each round both players choose privately: 🔫 Pistol, 🎯 Sniper, or 💥 Shotgun.
  4. Beats: Pistol beats Sniper, Sniper beats Shotgun, Shotgun beats Pistol. Same weapon = draw (no point).
  5. First to win 3 rounds wins the match. Winner takes the full pot if there was a wager. After the match you can click Rematch (no cooldown).

Round timeout 60s. No rematch cooldown.

Properties are bought from /menu → Shop → Properties. Some properties are required before you can use certain features.

Property Gates

  • Motorcycle Clubhouse — Required to buy MC businesses (weed, meth, cocaine, counterfeit, document forgery). Buy from Shop → Properties first.
  • Maze Bank Tower Office — Required to buy specialty cargo (vehicle warehouse, large cargo, cargo warehouse). Buy from Shop → Properties first.
  • Weapons Cage — Required to buy and use most weapons from the shop. Until you own it, the main menu does not show the Weapons button; weapon shop items that need it show as locked. Hustle payout can still be boosted by weapon levels once you have weapons.

Garage and other properties expand vehicle slots or unlock other systems; check the shop for descriptions.

Dual currency

Wallet — Stealable. Bank — Cannot be stolen. Keep spending cash in wallet, savings in bank.

Commands

/bank — Banking interface. /deposit <amount> and /withdraw <amount>; amounts support k, M, B, max.

Fees & limits

Transaction fee (e.g. 2% per deposit/withdraw); minimum withdrawal (e.g. $100). Plan transfers to avoid many small fees.

Strategy

Keep $500–$1K in wallet for expenses; deposit excess. Check status via Street Crime → Protection in /menu. Only wallet can be stolen — if you get robbed often, you kept too much in wallet.

Target another player’s wallet only; bank money is safe. Access via /menu → Steal or /steal @player and /mug @player.

  • Basics: Min target wallet ~$500+. Your RP helps; wanted can hurt. Cooldown 30 min (shared).
  • /steal — Base success rate around 60%, modified by reputation; you take 10–50% of target’s wallet.
  • /mug — Riskier, higher reward; around 30–80% of target’s wallet. Lower base success than steal.
  • Failure: Steal fail = lose ~10% wallet; mug fail = harsher. Use Street Crime → Protection in /menu. Bank savings; build RP.

Street Crime → Protection shows your status and theft threshold.

/heist or /menu → Heist opens Heist Central. Two entry points: ✅ Start (dynamic/interactive heists) and ⚡ Classic Heists (quick and major).

Dynamic Heists (Interactive)

Multi-stage mini-games. Each heist has a min rep and Heist Level requirement. Examples (values may vary by server):

  • Fleeca Job — L1, 0 rep | ~$8K–$20K | 30 min CD
  • Prison Break — L3, 100 rep | ~$20K–$40K | 1h CD
  • Series A Funding — L3, 200 rep | ~$25K–$50K | 1.5h CD
  • Humane Labs — L4, 300 rep | ~$35K–$70K | 2h CD
  • Pacific Standard — L5, 500 rep | ~$40K–$80K | 3h CD

If locked, the bot shows “Heist Locked” and the required level/rep. Major heists: crew 1–4 (success bonus, payout split); approach (Stealth/Smart/Loud) affects success and heat — Stealth often pays more, less heat. Heat reduces success; equipment paid up-front; cooldowns per type.

/business or main menu Business — Business Center. Types: MC (document forgery, weed, counterfeit, meth, coke), bunker, nightclub, vehicle warehouse, large cargo. Need Motorcycle Clubhouse for MC; Maze Bank Tower Office for specialty cargo (Shop → Properties).

Supplies & production

Supplies 0–5 blocks (~$15K per block). Production consumes supplies → stock. Restock can fail (lose half cost, 10 min cooldown, wanted). Upgrades: Equipment (1.5× speed), Staff (1.3×), Security (less raids, daily fees). Daily fees auto-deduct.

Selling

Local ~80% (low risk) · Los Santos 100% (high risk) · Split ~50% · Quick cashout ~80% instant. Nightclub: no supplies; technicians + warehouse; storage upgrades.

/casino or main menu Casino — Play blackjack, slots, and other games. Currency: chips. Buy and sell chips at the Cashier’s Cage (in the casino flow). You start with 1,000 chips after /start. Games: Blackjack, Slots, Coin Flip, Lucky Numbers, Roulette, Poker, 3 Card Poker, Dice, RPS, Connect 4, Tic-Tac-Toe. Diamond Casino property unlocks high-stakes (bet up to chip balance). Tips: Blackjack use basic strategy; Slots pure luck; Coin Flip 50/50. Set limits; gambling for fun, not income.

/shop or main menu Shop — Black Market. Most purchases use wallet cash.

Categories

  • Vehicles — Code-based; /buy car <code> (e.g. /buy car adder). Need garage slots (properties).
  • Properties — Clubhouse, Office, Weapons Cage, garage, etc. Unlock /business, /warehouse, /nightclub and weapons.
  • Weapons — /buy weapon <code>. Most need Weapons Cage; else locked.
  • Collectables — Luxury items. Boosters — Temporary bonuses.

Strategy: Invest in properties first; expand garages for more vehicles; don't drain wallet before a heist.

Daily reward

/daily — 24h cooldown. Streak: Consecutive days up to 7; bonus +10% per day (e.g. Day 7 = +60%). Miss 48h+ and streak resets. Base $500 with no businesses; $10,000 per business (then × streak). Money goes to wallet.

Other commands

/wanted — Wanted level. /leaderboard — Rankings (net worth, cash, chips, level, rep, bank). /profile — Full profile. /vote — Top.gg vote/review. Admins: /setlevelingchannel — Level-up announcements. /reset-tos — Reset user TOS (admin only).

Before You Accept TOS

Only /start works. All other commands reply with a message to accept the Terms first.

After You Accept TOS

  • Heists — Each heist has a Heist Level and/or min rep; the bot shows “Heist Locked” and requirements if you don’t qualify.
  • MC businesses — Require Motorcycle Clubhouse (Shop → Properties).
  • Specialty cargo (vehicle/cargo warehouses) — Require Maze Bank Tower Office (Shop → Properties).
  • Weapons / Weapons button — Most weapons and the main-menu Weapons button require the Weapons Cage property (Shop → Properties).
  • Stealing — Target must have at least ~$500 in wallet (configurable); 30 min cooldown between attempts.
  • Racing — You pay the entry fee; if you lose, you lose the fee.

No level or property is required to use /menu, /jobs, /bank, /heist (to view or run unlocked heists), /casino, /shop (browsing), /daily, or /hustle. Only the specific actions above are gated.
